How to stay warm:

• Wear layers of loose fitting clothes will keep you warmer than tight layers

• Designate a "warm room" for everyone to stay in and put towels, blankets, etc. under doors and over windows for insulation

• Keep hands and feet warm with socks and gloves. Wear a hat indoors if needed.

• Turn on ALL faucets so tiny drip is going to prevent pipes from bursting.

• Boil pots of water or warm up water bottles
